Hi, I'm Brandon.


I'm a media arts student from the School of Interactive Arts & Technology at Simon Fraser University. I love to communicate ideas visually through video and motion graphics, engaging the viewer as much as I can while peaking their interest as they watch through videos. For me, the best tool for educating is visual representation; and that's why I want to find new ways to educate people on different aspects of a concept. I'm also a big fan of video games, exercise, and Marvel.


I dream of being a part of an organization that cares a lot about its community and focuses on relaying messages through visual media. With this in mind, I envision a career that lets me lead a team to create meaningful video that resonates within each viewer.
